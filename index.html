About

At Dras-go we understand that boat ownership isn't for everyone. There are storage and maintenance costs and, of course, the cost of the boat itself! Some people just want to go sailing, and this is where Dras-go can help.

Book online or email to check availability. The fleet are available from April 1st through to and including 31st October, after which they are laid up for winter and a strict maintenance schedule is completed.

All of our boats have adequate levels of safety equipment onboard. RYA coding is not required for inland water, but we take safety very seriously. We require evidence of at least RYA Dayskipper theory and practical certificates or above (an ICC certificate is adequate) to charter any of our boats.
